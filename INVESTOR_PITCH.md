# PlotCare Investor Pitch

## 1. Title
**PlotCare: the activation layer for idle land**

Speaker note: PlotCare starts in Andhra Pradesh with absentee and urban landowners who own land but lack the visibility, trust, and operating workflow to activate it.

Proof needed: founder intro, launch geography, current stage.

## 2. Problem
**Land is valuable, but many owners cannot operate it remotely.**

Absentee owners often do not know exact plot condition, realistic use cases, trustworthy local operators, or how to track activity after handing land over.

Proof needed: interviews with AP landowners, examples of inherited/NRI/urban land ownership friction.

## 3. Supply-Demand Gap
**Owners have idle land. Farmers and operators need access. The match is missing trust.**

PlotCare is designed to sit between landowners and productive operators with a land intelligence report, verification workflow, and managed activation path.

Proof needed: farmer/operator interviews, land access demand, use-case willingness to pilot.

## 4. Solution
**A workflow from plot details to activation.**

1. Locate and profile the land.
2. Generate a Land Intelligence Report.
3. Match the right farmer or production partner.
4. Track activation and owner updates.

Proof needed: sample reports, workflow screenshots, report turnaround benchmarks.

## 5. Beachhead
**Start in Visakhapatnam and the Andhra Pradesh corridor.**

The wedge is narrow by design: urban and overseas AP landowners, local farmer/operator partners, and use cases such as cultivation, mushroom, nursery, aquaculture, and solar where suitable.

Proof needed: first 100 qualified landowner leads, district segmentation, partner pipeline.

## 6. Market Context
**The opportunity is visible in land fragmentation, fallow land, diaspora ownership, and rural energy/agri use cases.**

Source-backed reference points:
- MEA lists 35.4M overseas Indians.
- PIB/Agriculture Census 2015-16 lists 8.52M operational holdings in Andhra Pradesh.
- USDA/FAS cites 7% of India's agricultural land as fallow and more than 120M farms.
- MNRE PM-KUSUM supports solar plant and lease-rent pathways where land is suitable.

Proof needed: source appendix, AP-specific ownership and land-use estimates, bottom-up TAM/SAM/SOM.

## 7. Business Model
**Monetization begins with insight and expands into managed activation.**

Initial paths:
- Land Intelligence Reports.
- Landowner subscription for tracking and documents.
- Future platform commission on crop, lease, or operator workflows after pilots prove execution.

Proof needed: pricing tests, paid report conversion, willingness-to-pay interviews.

## 8. Moat
**Every report improves the plot intelligence and trust graph.**

Defensibility comes from the repeated workflow: plot data, owner intent, local partners, suitability history, document readiness, and district-level execution knowledge.

Proof needed: data model, repeatable verification process, operating playbook by district.

## 9. 90-Day Validation Plan
**Convert the pitch into measurable proof.**

Targets to define before investor meetings:
- Qualified landowner leads.
- Reports delivered.
- Farmer/operator partners screened.
- Pilot acres identified.
- LOIs or pilot agreements prepared.

Proof needed: actual metrics, conversion funnel, weekly validation dashboard.

## 10. Ask
**No funding amount shown yet.**

Current ask: investor feedback, customer introductions, district-level operating advisors, and pilot partner referrals. Add a specific funding ask only after validation milestones are real.

Proof needed: use-of-funds plan, milestones unlocked by capital, founder commitment.
